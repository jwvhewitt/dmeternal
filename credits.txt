Most tiles by the supremely talented (and generous) David Gervais. You can
download his tilesets from here:

    http://pousse.rapiere.free.fr/tome/

Part of the graphic tiles used in this program is the public domain roguelike
tileset "RLTiles". Some of the tiles have been modified by me.

    http://rltiles.sf.net

Also, the more recent Crawl tiles:

    http://git.develz.org/?p=crawl.git;a=summary

Nuclear explosion, Pearl effect by Jetrel.

Smoke, Steam, and K-T Event effects by Pow Studios. http://powstudios.com

Many other graphics obtained from http://opengameart.org


Programming mostly by Joseph Hewitt, except the bits I stole from other people.
